  1.                   SERVICES PROVIDED BY THE COMPANY:

 

2.1               Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Client hereby retains the Company as an independent service provider to provide end-to-end recruitments for the Client by placing Candidates (who have been selected and confirmed by the Client in the manner provided below), to the Client on a non exclusive basis, for carrying out the services and other particulars as set out in the Statement of Work (“Services”), and the Company agrees, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, to render such Services during the Term in the following manner :

2.1.1  Prior to engaging the Candidates, the CLIENT will share complete job description of  the potential Candidates to the Company.

2.1.2   The Company will share the profile of the potential Candidates with the Client. The final selection of the Candidates shall be undertaken and rest with the Client. The number of Candidates may be increased, subject to the demand / requirement from the Client, in which event a separate addendum/Schedule shall be executed by both the Parties in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.  

2.1.3    The Parties hereby expressly agrees that pursuant to the Effective Date, the Company shall execute a separate contractual agreement with the Candidates, which interalia shall set out details of the fees to be provided by the Company to such Candidates during the Term (“Fees”). Client will in no way interfere in-between the terms and conditions of the contract entered into between the Company and the Candidates, if any.

  1.                On remittance of the Fees to the Candidate, the Company shall be discharged from any further obligations in respect of any payments to the Candidate. Except as provided in Clauses above, the Company shall not be liable to pay to the Candidate any further amounts or fees.
  2.                The Company shall not exert any control over the projects and work assigned to the Candidates by the Client. The Company shall only have the limited responsibility of making  payments to the Candidates engaged by the CLIENT and will not direct and / or supervise (to any degree, directly or indirectly) the Candidates and / or the work allocated to them under this engagement. The Company will enable transference of “Best practices” for professionals to the Candidate to assist her in carrying out the services to be provided to the Client more effectively. The Company will also make itself available for informal feedback and modest interventions where   warranted between Client and the Candidate.
  3.                The Client understands and appreciates that the services provided by the Company or any of its Candidates are under the ambit and within the legal framework prescribed under the applicable laws.
  4.                In the event the Client is not satisfied with the performance/output of the Candidate assigned to it (“Original Candidate”), it shall notify the same to the Company, and the Company shall on a best effort basis replace the said Candidate with someone with relevant profile (“Replacement Candidates”) at no additional cost of recruitment.

2.6      That subject to Clause 2.5, if the Company provides a replacement of the Original Candidate, the Client hereby undertakes to clear all dues for the services rendered by the Original Candidate to the Company till the last day that the Original Candidate has worked with the Client, prior to the appointment of the Replacement Candidates.

  1.                The Company shall work with the Candidate to return to the Client any and all property belonging to the Client, documents, correspondence, records of the Client or created for or on behalf of the Client which are in the Candidate’s possession and/ or (b) hand over all final deliverables and/or assignments to the Client  subject to  the Client  paying all dues including the Consideration to the Company in full till the date of termination of the Agreement.

  1.                Any renewal of the Agreement shall be by mutual written agreement and shall be for such additional periods and on such terms and conditions as may be mutually agreed between the Parties in writing (including emails) at such point of time.

4.1.  In consideration of the Services rendered by the Company for each project, the Client shall pay the consideration amount set out in the Statement of Work (“Consideration”).

4.2.  The Consideration to be paid to the Company shall be separate for separate projects as may be required by the Client from time to time. All invoices will become due for payment as per the timelines mentioned in respective Statement of Works.

4.4 The Company will make payment to the Candidates upon receipt of remittance of the fees from the Client. Hence it is important to receive these remittances on time for the continued motivation of the Candidate(s) working with the Client.

4.5  If the CLIENT fails to pay THE COMPANY the Consideration (stipulated under respective Annexure(s)) for 30 (thirty) days from the due date, and the same is not rectified within 15 (fifteen) days, the said amount shall become immediately due and the Client shall pay the Company interest at such interest rate as specified by The Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development Act, 2006.

4.6 Notwithstanding anything contained above, the Company reserves its right to discontinue the Services until payment of all dues in accordance with this Agreement to the Company.

8         Non-Solicitation

 

8.1.1         The Parties mutually agree and undertake that during the Term and all extensions thereof, and  for 12 (twelve) months thereafter, neither Party will, except with prior written consent of the other Party, solicit for employment or consultation any individual  who at the time of such solicitation, is engaged as an employee, associate or consultant (other than the Candidate(s)) of the other Party, unless the Parties have executed this Agreement along with the Statement of Work. 

 

 

8.2  In the event, pursuant to the expiry of the Term, if the Client desires to continue availing the services of the Candidate, the Client shall intimate the Company regarding the same and the Parties shall renew the Agreement on mutual terms and conditions.

 

8.3 In the event however, if the Client directly recruits the Candidate without complying with Clause 8.2 set out above, the Company shall be entitled to charge a one time placement fee equivalent to one’s month’s salary/ fee to be paid by the Client to the Candidate (“Placement Fee”).

11    FORCE MAJEURE

11.1      Neither the Client nor the Company shall be liable to each other for, or be in breach of or default under this Agreement by reason of, any delay or failure to perform its obligations, under this Agreement which results  from any event of “force majeure” which shall include, without limitation, any act of God, civil war or strife, hostilities (whether war be declared or not), act of foreign enemy, invasion, war, rebellion, strikes, lockouts or other industrial disputes or actions, breakdown of facilities, legal enactment, lockdown declared by the Government, pandemic, epidemic, order or regulation or any other Governmental action or cause beyond its reasonable control and which such Party is unable to overcome by the exercise of reasonable diligence (“Force Majeure”).

11.2      If the performance by any Party of any of its obligations under this Agreement is affected by a Force Majeure Event for a continuous period of 7 (seven) days, such Party prevented from performing its obligations under this Agreement must immediately notify the other Party giving full particulars of the event of Force Majeure and the reasons for the event of Force Majeure preventing that Party from, or delaying that Party in performing its obligations under this Agreement ("FM Notice”) and that Party must use its reasonable efforts to mitigate the effect of the event of Force Majeure upon its performance of the Agreement and to fulfill its obligations under the Agreement.

11.3      Upon issuance of the FM Notice, either Party will be entitled to suspend the Agreement by issuing a notice of such suspension within 2 (two) Business Days of receiving or issuing the FM Notice.

11.4      Upon completion of the event of Force Majeure, the Party affected must as soon as reasonably practicable recommence the performance of its obligations under this Agreement.

11.5      An event of Force Majeure shall not relieve a Party from liability for an obligation which arose before the occurrence of the Force Majeure event, nor shall the Force Majeure event affect the obligation to pay money in a timely manner which matured prior to the occurrence of that Force Majeure event.

 

12    ARBITRATION

12.1      In the event any dispute arises between the Parties out of or in connection with this Agreement, including the validity thereof, the Parties hereto shall endeavor to settle such dispute amicably in the first instance. The attempt to bring about an amicable settlement shall be treated as having failed as soon as one of the Parties hereto, after reasonable attempts, which shall continue for not less than 15 (fifteen) days, gives a notice to this effect, to the other Party in writing.

12.2      In case of such failure, the dispute shall be referred to and resolved by arbitration to be conducted in accordance with the  (Indian) Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. Such Arbitration shall be conducted by a sole Arbitrator, who shall be mutually appointed by the Parties. In the event the Parties are unable to mutually agree on the appointment of the sole Arbitrator in the manner specified above, then the arbitrator shall be appointed by the competent courts of Mumbai in accordance with the provisions of the (Indian) Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996. The Arbitration proceedings shall be held in Mumbai and the language shall be English.
