  1.         SERVICES PROVIDED BY THE COMPANY:

 

  1.      Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Client hereby retains the Company as an independent service provider to provide end-to-end recruitments, on a non exclusive basis, for the Client by placing qualified and experienced women professionals across all areas of expertise who have been selected and confirmed by the Client in the manner provided below, for carrying out the services and other particulars as set out in the Statement of Work (“Services”), and the Company agrees, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, to render such Services during the Term in the following manner.
  2.      Prior to engaging the Candidates, the Client will provide the Company with all necessary details about the job description to be filled by the potential Candidates including role description, compensation range, skill sets, past experience, domain knowledge required, location constraints (“JD”).
  3.      Pursuant to receiving the JD, the Company shall undertake search and recruitment efforts in order to find a potential Candidate for the job role as set out in the JD.
  4.      The Company will share the profile of the potential Candidates with the Client. The final selection of the Candidates shall be undertaken and rest with the Client. The number of Candidates may be increased, subject to the demand / requirement from the Client, in which event a separate Statement of Work shall be executed by both the Parties in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.  
  5.      Pursuant to selection of the Candidate by the Client, the Company shall, after discussing the same with the Client, recommend a joining date that is 1-10 days (at maximum) from the final interview date, in order to retain the interest of the selected Candidates, subject to extraneous circumstances. Once the candidate is selected by the Client, the Client shall inform the Company of such selection including the compensation and joining date agreed with the Candidate.
  6.      The Client shall ensure that a contract is executed between the Client and the Candidate before the Candidate joins the Company. Such contract shall inter alia set out the following : (a) the scope of  work that the Candidate is required to carry out (b) the terms of arrangement (c) the term of the contract (if any) (a) details of the fees/ salary to be provided by the Company to such Candidates during the Term (“Fees”)(b) the disbursements that the Candidate shall be entitled to, (d) method of payment (e) Number of times she is required to visit the office of the Company. The Company will in no way interfere in-between the terms and conditions of the contract entered into between the Client and the Candidates, if any.
  7.      The Company assumes no responsibility on the output and progress and / or shall not exert any control over the projects and work assigned to the Candidates by the Client. The Company shall not direct and / or supervise (to any degree, directly or indirectly) the Candidates and / or the work allocated to them by the Client under this engagement.
  8.      The Client understands and appreciates that the services provided by the Company or any of its Candidates are under the ambit and within the legal framework prescribed under the Applicable Laws. The Company shall not be held liable for any act and / or omission of non – performance by the Candidate to provide any service under the Agreement.

3.1    Any resume or profile sent by the Company  to the Client shall be deemed to be a profile/ resume or               a candidate screened by the Client and shall be a valid screened resume or profile for a period of 6 (six) months from the date of receiving that resume or profile. During that period, if such candidate is hired by the Client it will be deemed as a candidate hired using the Company’s services and the terms of this Agreement will apply.

  1.      If the Client rejects a candidate's profile or resume sent by the Company. whether or not the recruitment has been for the specific JD, and later recruits that candidate within the profile validity period of 6 (six) from the date of profile being shared with the Client, it will be accounted as the Company’s placement.

 

  1.         Other Working Arrangement between Client and the Company :

 

4.1  In the event  any of the following occurs the Client will request the Company for a replacement and the Company shall replace the Candidate (“Original Candidate”) with a candidate of similar caliber (“Replacement Candidate”) at no additional cost within 4 (months) months of the Company notifying the Client that:-

i.  the Original Candidate has left the employment of the Candidate voluntarily;

ii.  the employment of the Original Candidate has been terminated on grounds of fraud, misrepresentation or moral turpitude.

iii.  the Original Candidate is found unfit for the job role by the Client or the Client is dissatisfied with her performance. However, in such cases the Client and the Company shall first exhaust all options for conciliation with the Original Candidate first which shall include options like giving her specific feedback & a fair chance to rectify/ reconcile her services. The Client shall provide the Company with adequate time to mediate, and try to make the relationship work with the Original Consultant first.

  1.      That subject to Clause 3.1, if the Company provides a replacement of the Original Candidate, the Client hereby undertakes to (a) clear all dues for the services rendered by the Original Candidate to the Company till the last day that the Original Candidate has worked with the Client, prior to the appointment of the Replacement Candidates and (b)  The Client shall ensure that a contract is executed between the Replacement Candidate and the Client in the manner set out in clause 2.6 above, before the Replacement Candidate joins the Company.
