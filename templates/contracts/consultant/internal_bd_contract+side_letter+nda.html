2.                  APPOINTMENT OF CONSULTANT

 

2.1 Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, the Company hereby appoints the Consultant, on a non-exclusive basis, as an independent consultant to provide the Consultancy Services to the Company, for the Term in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement and the Consultant hereby agrees to provide the Consultancy Services to the Company. During the Term, the Consultant may engage in or be connected with any other business or service except for a business or service that competes with the business of the Company. In the event the Consultant has any doubt on who the competitor of the Company is the Consultant shall speak to the Company, regarding the same.

2.2 The Consultant undertakes to apply its best efforts to ensure that the Consultancy Services are in conformity as per the best practices and standards prescribed by the Company.

 

3.                  Term

 

3.1  The Term of the Agreement has been set out in Schedule 3.  Any renewal of the Agreement shall be by mutual written agreement and shall be for such additional periods and on such terms and conditions as may be mutually agreed between the Parties in writing (including emails) at such point of time.             

 

4.                  Obligations of Consultant

 

4.1 During the Term, the Consultant will provide the Company with the Consultancy Services. Subject to the provisions of this Agreement, the Consultancy Services may be amended mutually between the Parties in writing.

4.2 The Consultant shall at all times during rendering its services under this Agreement, adhere to Applicable Law for rendering the Consultancy Services.

4.3 The Consultant shall render the Consultancy Services under or pursuant to this Agreement, diligently, professionally, faithfully in a timely fashion and to the best of her ability and shall not engage in any activity which shall directly or indirectly conflict with the interests of the Company.

4.4 The Consultant shall keep the Company  informed about all developments, problems, delays or any other impediments that may affect the Consultant in providing the Consultancy Services in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement. In the event the Consultant is unable to render the Consultancy Services at any point during the Term, including for a Medical Emergency, the Consultant shall intimate the Authorised Representative of the Company  immediately.

 

5.                  Arrangement between the Company and the Consultant:

 

5.1 The Consultant will perform the Consultancy Services for the Company remotely. The Consultant shall however be required to visit the Company’s office at such time and date as mutually agreed between the Parties in Schedule 6.  

5.2      During the process of rendering Consultancy Service, the Consultant shall interact or associate with the Authorised Representative of the Company,

5.3      The Consultant shall cooperate with the Company during the induction procedure carried out by the Company in every respect to ensure that such procedure is carried out seamlessly.

5.4 The Company shall be responsible for paying the Consultancy Fee as outlined in Clause 6 of this Agreement to the Consultant.

 

6.                  Monthly Consultancy Fee and payment method

 

6.1      In consideration of carrying out the Consultancy Services in accordance with this Agreement, the Company shall pay the Consultant Monthly Consultancy Fee as per Schedule 4, subject to TDS as required by Applicable Law.

6.2 Subject to the provisions of this Clause 6, the Monthly Consultancy Fee shall be payable on a monthly basis by the Company in the manner set out in Schedule 4.

6.3 On such remittance, the Company shall be discharged from any further obligations towards the Consultant in respect of payment referred to above. Except as provided in Clauses above, the Company shall not be liable to pay to the Consultant any further amounts or fees.

6.4 All payments to the Consultant will be made in INR and net of transfer fees, charges or other costs associated with such transfer. 

6.5 The Consultant shall provide all necessary or desirable assistance to the Company in relation to the remittance of payments referred to above.

6.6 All payments made to the Consultant by the Company including Disbursements have been set out in Schedule 7 and the Company shall not be responsible for making these payments.

 

7.                  Covenants

 

7.1 The Consultant shall at all times render high standards of Consultancy Services and  ensure proper care and exercise independent professional judgment.

7.2 The Consultant agrees that the Company may withhold the information/ documents of any nature provided by the Consultant, even after the termination of the Agreement and may use such information for the conduct of its business in India or outside India.

7.3 The Consultant shall not, directly or indirectly, make any statement or become privy to any act, practice or competition, which is harmful or is likely to be harmful to the interest of the Company and/ or the Consultant.

7.4 The Consultant agrees that this Agreement is personal in nature and she shall not delegate or assign her obligations and rights under this Agreement to any third party.

7.5 It is expressly agreed between the Parties herein that the Company shall not be required to provide the Consultant with a laptop/ computer and/ or with internet for carrying out her duties under this Agreement and neither will Company be required to reimburse the costs for the laptop/ computer and/ or the internet services used by Consultant for carrying out her duties under this Agreement. The Consultant shall use her own laptop/ computer and pay for the internet services required for her to carry out her duties under this Agreement.

11.7  To adhere to standards of communication including periodic reviews of work done as agreed with co-founders or other designated senior management team as applicable.

i.               undergoes to discharge her responsibilities under this Agreement through some other person;

ii.               engages in a conduct which is likely to tarnish or damage the reputation of the Company or the Company;

iii.               pays or accepts any Improper Payments

iv.               is accused or convicted of, plead guilty to, or admitted committing, a felony or any offense involving dishonesty or deception (e.g., theft, fraud, etc.);

vi.               is subject to an order, judgment, action, or investigation of a court or any national or state regulatory or self-regulatory organization;

viii.               defaults any of the terms under this Agreement.

(b) which was known to the Party, as evidenced by its written records, prior to it receiving such Confidential Information;

16.6 If the Company requires the Consultant to enter into a separate non disclosure agreement with the Company, the Consultant hereby agrees to comply with such requirement.  

16.7 The Consultant shall maintain utmost confidentiality of the terms of this Agreement including her Consultancy Fee/ Monthly Consultancy Fee, and shall not discuss any part of the same with the Company or any third party.

21                ARBITRATION

 

21.1 (a) In the event any dispute arises between the Parties out of or in connection with this Agreement, including the validity thereof, the Parties hereto shall endeavor to settle such dispute amicably in the first instance. The attempt to bring about an amicable settlement shall be treated as having failed as soon as one of the Parties hereto, after reasonable attempts, which shall continue for not less than 15 (fifteen) days, gives a notice to this effect, to the other Party in writing.

(b) In case of such failure, the dispute shall be referred to a sole Arbitrator, who shall be mutually appointed by the Parties. In the event the Parties are unable to mutually agree on the identity of the sole Arbitrator in the manner specified above, then the arbitration proceedings shall be conducted by the three Arbitrators of which the first Arbitrator shall be appointed by the Company, the second Arbitrator by the Consultant and both such arbitrators appointed shall mutually appoint the third presiding Arbitrator. The Arbitration proceedings shall be governed by the (Indian) Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 and shall be held in New Delhi, India and the language of arbitration shall be English.

(c) The seat, venue or legal place of arbitration shall be Mumbai and any award shall be treated as an award made at the seat of the arbitration. The arbitration proceedings shall be conducted in the English language.

21.2 The arbitrator’s award shall be a reasoned award and shall be in writing. The arbitrators shall also decide on the costs of the arbitration proceedings.

21.3 Subject to the award of the sole arbitrator, neither the existence of any Dispute nor the fact that any arbitration is pending hereunder shall relieve any of the Parties of their respective obligations under this Agreement. Subject to any award of the sole arbitrator, the pendency of a Dispute in any arbitration proceeding shall not affect the performance of the obligations under this Agreement.

21.4 The provisions of this Clause 21 shall survive the termination of this Agreement for any reason whatsoever.

(ii) solicit, entice or attempt to solicit or entice, directly or indirectly, any Person who is or has been at any time in the last 24 (twenty four) months from the relevant date, a customer/client of the Company;

(iii)                        solicit, hire or entice away or endeavour to solicit, hire or entice away, directly or indirectly, any person (whether employee or director) employed or engaged by the Company and/ or its Affiliates; or

(iv)                        cause or permit any Person directly or indirectly to do any of the foregoing acts or things.
